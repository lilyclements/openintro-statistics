Definition 5.3.6.

When the p-value is less than the significance level \(\alpha\text{,}\) we reject \(H_0\) and report that the data provide strong evidence supporting the alternative hypothesis.
When the p-value is greater than \(\alpha\text{,}\) we do not reject \(H_0\text{,}\) and we report that we do not have sufficient evidence to reject the null hypothesis.
