Theorem 6.2.1. Sampling Distribution of \(\hat{p}_1 - \hat{p}_2\).

When the following conditions are satisfied, the sampling distribution of \(\hat{p}_1 - \hat{p}_2\) is nearly normal with:
\begin{gather*} \end{gather*}
Conditions:
  1. Independence: The data in each group must be independent, and the two groups must be independent of each other.
  2. Success-failure condition: The success-failure condition must hold for each sample: \(n_1p_1 \geq 10\text{,}\) \(n_1(1-p_1) \geq 10\text{,}\) \(n_2p_2 \geq 10\text{,}\) and \(n_2(1-p_2) \geq 10\text{.}\)
