The 52 cards are split into four suits: \(\clubsuit\) (club), \(\diamondsuit\) (diamond), \(\heartsuit\) (heart), \(\spadesuit\) (spade). Each suit has its 13 cards labeled: 2, 3, â€¦, 10, J (jack), Q (queen), K (king), and A (ace). Thus, each card is a unique combination of a suit and a label, e.g. \(4\heartsuit\) and \(J\clubsuit\text{.}\) The 12 cards represented by the jacks, queens, and kings are called face cards. The cards that are \(\diamondsuit\) or \(\heartsuit\) are typically colored red while the other two suits are typically colored black.
