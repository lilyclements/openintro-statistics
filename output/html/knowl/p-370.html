Above we write \(p\) as the probability of rolling a 1. We can also write this probability as
\begin{equation*} P(\\text{rolling a 1}) \end{equation*}
As we become more comfortable with this notation, we will abbreviate it further. For instance, if it is clear that the process is “rolling a die”, we could abbreviate \(P(\)rolling a 1\()\) as \(P(1)\text{.}\)
