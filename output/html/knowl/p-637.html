We can add up the heights of the bins in the range 180 cm and 185 cm and divide by the sample size. For instance, this can be done with the two shaded bins shown in Figure 3.5.4. The two bins in this region have counts of 195,307 and 156,239 people, resulting in the following estimate of the probability:
\begin{equation*} \frac{195307 + 156239}{3,000,000} = 0.1172 \end{equation*}
This fraction is the same as the proportion of the histogram’s area that falls in the range 180 to 185 cm.
