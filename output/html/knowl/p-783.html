Exercise 4.1.6.7 states that average daily high temperature in June in LA is 77°F with a standard deviation of 5°F, and it can be assumed that they follow a normal distribution. We use the following equation to convert °F (Fahrenheit) to °C (Celsius):
\begin{equation*} C = (F - 32) \times \frac{5}{9} \end{equation*}
