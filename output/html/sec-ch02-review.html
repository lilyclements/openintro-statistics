    Section 2.4 Review exercises

    Exercises Review exercises

    1. Make-up exam.

    Class of 25: first 24 scored mean 74 (SD 8.9). Make-up student scored 64. Does mean increase/decrease? New mean? Effect on SD?

    2. Infant mortality.

    Histogram of infant deaths per 1,000 live births for 224 countries.
    1. Estimate Q1, median, Q3.
    2. Is mean likely smaller or larger than median?

    3. TV watchers.

    AP Stats class: mean 4.71 hrs, SD 4.18 hrs per week. Is distribution symmetric? Likely shape?

    4. A new statistic.

    For $x_i>0$, interpret distribution shape when $\bar x / \text{median}$ equals 1, less than 1, greater than 1.

    5. Oscar winners.

    Histograms and summary stats for age of Best Actor vs. Actress winners (1929‚Äì2018).
    Compare distributions.

    6. Exam scores.

    History exam mean 85, SD 15 (out of 100). Is distribution symmetric? Likely shape?

    7. Stats scores.

    Twenty scores listed; create a box plot (five-number summary provided).

    8. Marathon winners.

    Histogram and box plot of NYC Marathon winning times (1970‚Äì1999) plus gender boxplots and time series.
    1. Histogram vs. box plot: what each shows?
    2. Why bimodal overall distribution?
    3. Compare men vs. women times (boxplots).
    4. What does the time series reveal?
